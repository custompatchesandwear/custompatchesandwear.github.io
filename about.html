We are Glasgow Based Startup

In the heart of Glasgow, a vibrant city known for its rich history and arts scene, a new venture was born in 2024. This start-up, named ‘Custom Patches and Wear’ our company vision is to help people express their unique identities through custom patches, screen-printed hoodies, T-shirts, and leather jackets.

At Custom Patches and Wear, our aim extends beyond individual expression. We are dedicated to supporting companies and individuals who aspire to build their own brands. Whether you're a budding entrepreneur looking to launch a clothing line or a business seeking to strengthen your corporate identity, we provide the tools and expertise to bring your vision to life.
